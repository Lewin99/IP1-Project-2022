ALU, Course: Computing

My name is Lewis Ndahinyuka, and I was born and raised in Kigali, Rwanda. I'm a professional software engineer with expertise working with a variety of frameworks and programming languages. I'm interested programming in general and penetration testing. I have worked with languages such as C and C++, Java, python, and JavaScript, and I'm also specializing in React development, Since I'm a good designer, I like to focus on front-end development in my regular software development projects. However, I have plans to expand my back-end skills and possibly become a full stack developer.

In addition to programming, I enjoy playing with networking devices, which inspired me to study various networking tools and begin my hacking journey. At the moment, I'm attempting to teach myself various penetration techniques, which I will be employing with Python in my regular penetration testing activities.

African Leadership University (ALU)

Why ALU?

When I was looking for a university, ALU was my ideal choice since it had a strong commitment to changing Africa by developing young Africans into future leaders. I was always saddened by how other continents perceive Africa and wanted to do my tiny part to help change that. Thatâ€™s what inspired me to apply to ALU

Application Process!

Before attending ALU, I frequently enrolled in short courses to keep myself in the software engineering field. I heard about ALU from a friend and decided to apply, the tuition was a bit higher than I could afford, so I decided to defer my application. Later, in 2020, I heard about scholarships offered by ALU, so I reapplied, finally I was awarded a full scholarship. honestly I wasn't expecting to be chosen,i was all good memories.

Travelling to ALU

It was difficult to travel to ALU during those difficult years (19). Different paperwork are required to enter each country, and we were not permitted to leave our houses throughout the Covid period, It was difficult to obtain the paperwork since so many of them required you to participate in the procedure in person, but I was able to obtain them. Other than those difficulties, it was a wonderful experience. I was quite happy because it was my first time traveling, but the experience was not exactly what I had anticipated but it was good.

The ALU Experience!

My time at ALU and at Mauritius in general have been incredible; I've made many friends who we share the same desire to see our continent changed, and I've also learned a lot from first year, including life experience, work experience, and a lot that helped me develop a strong mindset. I would say that my time at ALU has been beneficial overall, I couldn't complain

Advice to prospective students!

ALU is a wonderful university that inspires you to become the future version of yourself, the version of yourself that you always imagine. It is a university that encourages you to have a mission and supports you as you pursue your mission. ALU is the ideal place for you if you want to change your community, your continent, or maybe the entire world. It is a university that is full with opportunity and deeply passionate about transforming our continent.
